Status Indicator

The TubeShift status indicator tells you if the extension is operating and if any alternative locations for the video being viewed are available.

Inactive

TubeShift is not performing any lookups for the current page.

Active

TubeShift is awaiting results from a lookup or there are no alternatives known for the current video. Anonymous information is being supplied to the TubeShift servers.

Available

There are alternate videos available. Clicking on the status indicator will show you all of the available alternatives.
