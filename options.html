Privacy

Anonymous Data Collection

In order to function this extension must send the following anonymous information to the TubeShift servers:

  • The identifiers of videos or channels on websites such as YouTube, Rumble, and DailyMotion.
  • The identifiers of content entries associated with those previously mentioned video identifiers.
You can turn off this anonymous data collection but this extension will no longer have any functionality.
